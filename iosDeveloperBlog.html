iOS Swift Developers Blog

Dear Paul is the name of my blog.

Paul is one of he many people that has been helping me.

Paul, a local business owner of an app and web developer copany. He suggested I write to him every Friday for accountability.

I've been e-mailing, texting and talking to other developers too and I am grateful to them all!

I've been using Slack, Messages and even Google hangouts to talk about code, ask questions and screen share my code.

I'm not sure why Messages works better for screen sharing than Facetime, it just does.

As a developer, we are told to write. For now, I'm taking the low barrier to entry route.

I'm just copying and pasting the content of my e-mails with minimal tweeking.

I may go back and do some pre-quil entries, but by the time I get to that, I may not care.

Thanks to all of the poople that have showed me support and gone out of their way for me.

This blog may not be sexy or pretty, but it's here and that is half the battle.

I got out of bed this morning and I started working and that is half the battle.

So let's Start off right! "Hello World"
